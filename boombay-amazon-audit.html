06 — Plan of Action

Six steps, in sequence.

The recommended path forward, built off the observations above.

1 Search Term Mining & Optimization Accelerate growth through continuous search term mining, keyword harvesting, and strategic negation to improve conversion efficiency and reduce wasted spend.
2 Target Expansion & Scale Accelerate reach by expanding into long-tail generic and competitor keyword themes, while scaling proven targets to drive incremental sales and improve new customer acquisition efficiency.
3 Efficiency-Based Campaign Structuring Restructure video and banner campaigns by segmenting high-, medium-, and low-CPC keywords into dedicated campaign buckets to improve bidding control, budget allocation, and scaling efficiency.
4 Remarketing Acceleration Strengthen remarketing by prioritizing recent shoppers, product viewers, and cart abandoners to improve repeat purchases and drive faster conversion cycles suited for FMCG consumption behavior.
5 Scale Category Targeting Expand category targeting to capture untapped demand, improve category penetration, and increase share of voice while maintaining efficient ACOS and sustainable growth.
6 AMC Audience-Based Expansion Test and scale AMC audience-based targeting with greater focus on ATC, browse, and engagement audiences beyond purchase cohorts to unlock incremental conversions and improve audience expansion opportunities.
